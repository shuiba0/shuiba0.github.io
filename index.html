I am a final-year student in a joint program at the University of Glasgow and University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) majoring in Electronic Information Engineering, and a research intern at Westlake NLP lab in Westlake University, advised by Prof. Yue Zhang . Before coming there, I was advised by Prof. Shuyuan Zhu (UESTC), focusing on image alignment and video impainting. I have been exploring the field of AI for a year, during which I have learned a lot about AI and tried some research topics both CV and NLP. I have researched into some low-level tasks in CV such as video impainting and image alignment. In NLP, I focus on the Explainable AI and the generalization of pre-trained language models. Recently I'm interested in generative models especially in diffusion model, and I'm looking for a RA position in the area.
